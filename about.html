OUR MISSION

The mission or Children's Creative Center is to provide a liearning environment to equip each child with the knowledge, skills, and values to be a successful learner. We are committed to provide a highly comprehensive program that supports the optimal development of the whole child. Our belief is that children learn through play.

We strive to provide an inclusive environment that fulfills each child's social, emotional, physical, and intillectual needs appropriate to the child's age and stage. Our focus is on the family because parents are the principal influence in a child's development. Our goal is to have children, parents, and teachers working together as a team. Our commitment is to the community. We bring families, teachers, and community members together in the spirit of teaching our children the value of cooperation, respect, and responsibility.
